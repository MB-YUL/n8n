# Academic Sources


### ArXiv (Computation and Language)

Preprint feed for the Computation and Language (`cs.CL`) category on arXiv, featuring the latest NLP and LLM research papers.

**Link:** https://rss.arxiv.org/rss/cs.CL

**Description:** Daily updates of new NLP/LLM preprints (before peer review).

- **Language:** English (papers may vary).
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Academic preprint feed.

### ArXiv (Machine Learning)

ArXiv feed for the Machine Learning (`cs.LG`) category, covering general AI/ML research including many LLM and generative model papers.

**Link:** https://rss.arxiv.org/rss/cs.LG

**Description:** Daily stream of new machine learning preprints (many relevant to generative AI).

- **Language:** English (primarily).
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Academic preprint feed.

### ArXiv Sanity Preserver

Tool by Andrej Karpathy for tracking and filtering arXiv papers. Helps surface relevant new AI papers with similarity search and user libraries.

**Link:** http://www.arxiv-sanity.com/

**Alternate Link:** https://arxiv-sanity-lite.com/

**Description:** Aggregates arXiv AI papers with recommendations, making it easier to find new LLM research.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Continuous.
- **Content Type:** Academic aggregator/tool.

### Papers with Code

Open repository of AI papers, code, and leaderboards. Also offers a weekly newsletter tracking trending papers.

**Link:** https://paperswithcode.com/

**Description:** Tracks the latest research and benchmarks in AI, with summaries of trending ML/LLM papers.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily site updates (weekly digest available).
- **Content Type:** Research portal and newsletter.
