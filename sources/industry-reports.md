# Industry Reports Sources


### State of AI Report

Annual report by Nathan Benaich and Ian Hogarth analyzing AI research, industry, policy, and safety trends.

**Link:** https://www.stateof.ai/

**Description:** Comprehensive annual synthesis of major AI developments and directional trends.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Annual.
- **Content Type:** Industry analysis report.

### Stanford AI Index

Yearly report by Stanford HAI tracking global AI progress across research, education, economy, and policy.

**Link:** https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index

**Description:** Data-driven benchmark report used by policymakers, researchers, and industry leaders.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Annual.
- **Content Type:** Data and metrics report.

### McKinsey Global Survey on AI (State of AI)

Annual McKinsey survey and analysis of enterprise AI adoption and business impact.

**Link:** https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai

**Description:** Survey-driven view of enterprise AI usage, scaling barriers, and value realization.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Annual.
- **Content Type:** Industry survey report.
