# Company Sources


### OpenAI Blog

Official OpenAI blog for model launches, research updates, safety notes, and policy context.

**Link:** https://openai.com/blog

**Description:** Primary source for GPT model releases and OpenAI research announcements.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Company research blog.

### Google AI Blog

Official Google AI blog for research breakthroughs and responsible AI updates.

**Link:** https://ai.googleblog.com/

**Description:** Coverage of Google AI research and product-level AI applications.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly or ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Company research blog.

### DeepMind Blog

Official DeepMind blog sharing research and technical stories from DeepMind teams.

**Link:** https://www.deepmind.com/blog

**Description:** Deep dives into major breakthroughs across science and AI systems.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Regular (roughly monthly or more).
- **Content Type:** Company research blog.

### Meta AI Blog

Official Meta AI blog covering generative AI, vision, NLP, and open model work.

**Link:** https://ai.meta.com/blog

**Description:** Research and engineering updates, often with open-source releases.

- **Language:** English (some multilingual posts).
- **Update Frequency:** Regular.
- **Content Type:** Company research/engineering blog.

### Microsoft AI Blog

Microsoft's official AI blog for product announcements and Microsoft Research developments.

**Link:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/blog

**Description:** Enterprise and product AI updates with links to broader research initiatives.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Frequent.
- **Content Type:** Company news and research blog.

### Anthropic Blog

Official Anthropic blog focused on Claude updates, safety research, and governance.

**Link:** https://www.anthropic.com/index/blog

**Description:** Guidance and research posts centered on reliable and steerable language models.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Company blog.

### Stability AI Blog

Official Stability AI blog for open generative model releases and project updates.

**Link:** https://stability.ai/blog

**Description:** Announcements and technical posts on model families and ecosystem collaborations.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Regular.
- **Content Type:** Company blog.
