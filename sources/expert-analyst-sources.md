# Expert and Analyst Sources


### Gary Marcus (Marcus on AI)

Substack by Gary Marcus focused on AI limits, safety, and policy critique.

**Link:** https://garymarcus.substack.com/

**Description:** Independent commentary aimed at separating durable signal from hype.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly-ish.
- **Content Type:** Expert commentary blog.

### SemiAnalysis (Dylan Patel)

Deep-dive analysis on AI economics, semiconductors, infrastructure, and scaling dynamics.

**Link:** https://semianalysis.com/

**Description:** High-detail analyst pieces on compute, supply chain, and AI business structure.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly or biweekly.
- **Content Type:** Analyst blog.

### Stratechery (Ben Thompson)

Tech strategy newsletter that frequently covers AI market dynamics and platform shifts.

**Link:** https://stratechery.com/

**Description:** In-depth business strategy analysis with regular AI-focused essays.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Multiple times per week.
- **Content Type:** Tech analysis newsletter/blog.

### Chip Huyen's Blog

Practical ML/LLM engineering blog focused on production deployment and system design.

**Link:** https://huyenchip.com/

**Description:** Technical essays bridging model research and production implementation.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Expert blog.

### AI Snake Oil

Newsletter and blog by Arvind Narayanan and Sayash Kapoor on AI limits and risk.

**Link:** https://aisnakeoil.substack.com/

**Description:** Evidence-based critiques of overclaims and misuse patterns in AI.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Regular (often biweekly).
- **Content Type:** Expert analysis newsletter.
