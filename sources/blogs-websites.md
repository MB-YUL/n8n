# Blogs and Websites Sources


### MarkTechPost

AI news platform covering new research, model launches, and practical technical breakdowns.

**Link:** https://www.marktechpost.com/

**Description:** Fast-moving AI research news and tutorial-style explainers for practitioners.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Blog/news site.

### Hugging Face Community Blog

Official Hugging Face community blog for open-source ML and LLM developments.

**Link:** https://huggingface.co/blog

**Description:** Tutorials, engineering guides, release announcements, and community contributions.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly or ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Company/community blog.

### BAIR Blog (Berkeley AI Research)

Academic lab blog from UC Berkeley AI Research.

**Link:** https://bair.berkeley.edu/blog

**Description:** Research-focused articles explaining new AI techniques and results from lab teams.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Ad hoc.
- **Content Type:** Research lab blog.

### The Gradient

AI online magazine for long-form analysis, interviews, and commentary.

**Link:** https://thegradient.pub/

**Description:** In-depth AI writing plus a recurring curated update on recent research and news.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Biweekly newsletter and ongoing articles.
- **Content Type:** Online magazine/blog.
