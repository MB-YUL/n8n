# Newsletter Sources


### The Rundown AI

Daily AI industry newsletter founded by Rowan Cheung. Covers major developments in generative AI, LLM announcements, and product launches in a concise format.

**Link:** https://www.therundown.ai/

**Description:** One of the largest independent AI newsletters, delivering quick and actionable updates on AI trends and tools.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### Ben's Bites

Daily no-fluff AI news roundup by Ben Tossell. Popular with startup founders and developers for brevity and practical focus.

**Link:** https://www.bensbites.com/

**Description:** Bite-sized updates on AI research, product news, and tools with a founder-centric perspective.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### Superhuman AI

Curated by Zain Kahn, focused on helping readers stay current on AI in minutes. Emphasizes productivity and business use cases.

**Link:** https://www.superhuman.ai/

**Description:** Quick-read updates on AI advancements and practical use cases for professionals.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### The Batch (deeplearning.ai)

Weekly AI newsletter led by Andrew Ng with major research, industry developments, and commentary.

**Link:** https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/

**Description:** Weekly digest of key AI news and research with detailed, accessible summaries.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### Import AI

Weekly newsletter by Jack Clark (Anthropic co-founder), focused on research and policy context.

**Link:** https://importai.substack.com/

**Alternate Link:** https://jack-clark.net/

**Description:** Long-running AI newsletter with detailed analysis of recent research and societal implications.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### TLDR AI

Part of the TLDR newsletter network, focused on ultra-brief summaries of AI news and research.

**Link:** https://tldr.tech/ai

**Description:** Short daily updates distilling AI news and papers for engineers and data scientists.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Daily (weekdays).
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### AlphaSignal

Weekly research-focused AI newsletter by researchers, with coverage of frontier model and hardware trends.

**Link:** https://alphasignal.ai/

**Description:** Technical briefing with analysis on model architectures, tooling, and compute trends.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter.

### Chain of Thought (Every)

Weekly AI column by Dan Shipper exploring implications of AI on work and creativity.

**Link:** https://every.to/chain-of-thought

**Description:** Essays and interviews linking technical AI progress to strategic and societal impact.

- **Language:** English.
- **Update Frequency:** Weekly.
- **Content Type:** Newsletter/column.
